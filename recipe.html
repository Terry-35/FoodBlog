Recipe Title

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado
  • 2 slices of wholegrain bread
  • Salt, pepper, and olive oil

Instructions

  1. Mash the avocado.
  2. Toast the bread.
  3. Spread the avocado on the toast and season to taste.
